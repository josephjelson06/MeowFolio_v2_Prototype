ABOUT RESUMEAI

A lightweight resume workflow built for repeated applications, not one-off uploads.

This prototype separates the public entry experience from the authenticated workspace. The goal is simple: make parsing, versioning, ATS review, and JD matching feel like one continuous loop.

What the free plan keeps visible

Profile essentials, usage details, limited daily parsing, and rule-based ATS/JD checks so the core loop is still understandable without paid complexity.

What the workspace is optimized for

Resume version libraries, saved JDs, quick comparisons, and focused reports that help you decide what to rewrite next.

How auth works here

The public surface exposes a Google-only sign in modal. Entering the workspace takes you directly to the existing post-auth dashboard flow.

Where this prototype is opinionated

It favors visible structure over hidden automation: paginated lists, editable names, parsed-text previews, and reports you can inspect before acting.

System split

Pre-auth for trust. Post-auth for work.

The landing and about pages explain the product and access path, while dashboard, resumes, JDs, editor, and profile remain the working app once the user continues with Google.
