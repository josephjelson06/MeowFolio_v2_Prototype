FAQ

Got questions?

Is it really free?
The current prototype keeps the public promise simple: the free plan stays visible, parsing is capped daily, and there is no paywall layer on these entry pages.
Does it actually generate PDFs now?
Inside the workspace, the flow is already built around TeX-backed output, preview, and ATS/JD checks. The public pages are explaining that same loop rather than pretending it is a separate tool.
Why keep the split workspace?
The landing and about pages are for trust, orientation, and sign-in. The dashboard, resumes, editor, JDs, and profile pages remain the actual working product once the user continues with Google.
What carries across the app?
The same resume content flows through editing, preview, ATS scoring, JD analysis, saved libraries, and exports. That shared state is the core product decision behind resumeai.
